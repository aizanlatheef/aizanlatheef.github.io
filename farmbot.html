FarmBot

07 March, 2018

First Arrival

The Farmbot kit finally arrived after a long time!


15 March, 2018

Checking out the kit

We opened the kit to checked if all of the parts and pieces had arrived.


19 March, 2018

Measuring the box

We moved the box up to it's location and measured it for attaching the rails later.


21 March, 2018

Starting Assembly

Today we put the metal plates onto the base of FarmBot. We attached them with wood screws and these plates are what hold up the whole of FarmBot to the wooden base.


22 March, 2018

Finishing the base of Farmbot

We finished attaching the rails to base of the Farmbot. Luckily it all lined up flush with minimal errors. The rails attached to the metal plates with screws and T-nuts.


26 March, 2018

Attaching the cable carriers

Today we began attaching the x-axis cable carriers. We also started constructing the base plates that slide along the tracks.


29 March - 6 April, 2018

Building the Gantry

Today we properly started to build the gantry. The gantry is the top bar which slides along the x-axis rails. We attached the sides of the gantry to the sliding base plate with T-nuts and screws. Attaching these were annoying because of the awkward angle we had to work with.

After constructing the two sides, we attached them together with the top bar. When we completed this, we slid it onto the rails. When we slid it on, it was very tight and we did not know why. We attached the belts to the gantry and it was complete.


9 April, 2018

Eccentric Spacers

Today we realised how eccentric spacers work. Eccentric spacers adjust the gap between the wheels and how they attach on the metal plates. This allows the wheels to slide freely on the rails, and it fixed the problem of the gantry not sliding properly.


6 August, 2018

Fixing the cable

After a long wait, the longer vacuume cable arrived and we installed it into Farmbot with some difficulty.


30 April - 3 March, 2018

Constructing the Z-axis

We began constructing the z-axis of the Farmbot. The z-axis is the part that goes up and down and will eventually have the tool mount on the end. While doing this, we also started sorting out the cables for the cable carriers to go onto the z-axis.

After we complete the z-axis, we attached the cables and the cable carriers to it and it was done.
